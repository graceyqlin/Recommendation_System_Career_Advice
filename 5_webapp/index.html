Career Advice Recommendation System (CARS)

A MIDS Capstone Project by Lucy Xie, Andrew Kabatznick, Jim Bauserman, and Grace Lin

CARS uses data on thousands of questions asked by jobseekers like you and real-life advice from specialists to help you find the answers you need to guide your future career. Just enter a question in the box below and click "Submit" to get a quick summary of what advisors have said about that topic along with some statistics about potential occupations.
